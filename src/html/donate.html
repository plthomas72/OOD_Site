Donate

Why Donate:

Opportunity On Deck is completely funded by our sponsors and donaters. Each and every dollar donated goes straight to organization, no salaries are paid. By donating to Opportunity on deck you can provide uniforms, sports equipment, and much more.

How to Donate:

Simply click our Dwolla Donate now button and you will be directed to the Dwolla site to complete the donation. You will have to create a Dwolla account and attach your bank account to donate.

Why do I need to create a Dwollla account?

Dwolla is a fast and convenient way to donate money. It has lower costs compared to paypal and credit card processing services. This allows more of your money to go to the participants of Opportunity on Deck. Creating an account is quick and easy. All you need is your bank account information and you are ready to go!
